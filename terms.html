CronometerClone — Terms of Use & Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

CronometerClone ("the app") is a personal health and nutrition tracker. With your explicit authorization, the app can connect to your health record (via MyChart / SMART-on-FHIR) to display your labs, vitals, conditions, medications, allergies, and immunizations.

How your data is handled

  • You control access. The app only reads data after you sign in to your health system and approve sharing. You can disconnect at any time from within the app.
  • Read-only. The app never modifies your medical record.
  • Stored on your device. Your health information and sign-in tokens are kept on your own device (iOS Keychain / local storage). The app does not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties.
  • Revoking access. Disconnecting in the app removes the stored tokens; you can also revoke access from your MyChart account settings.

No medical advice

The app is for personal informational use only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
